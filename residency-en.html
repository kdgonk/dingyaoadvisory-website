South Africa Permanent Residency (PR) — The High-Value Plan B.

Why South African Residency?

Compared to the high investment thresholds and strict stay requirements of US/EU countries, South Africa offers a more flexible and cost-effective choice.

Low Cost Entry

No need for millions in donations or risky investments. Apply via asset proof or stable passive income.

Minimal Stay Requirement

After obtaining PR, only minimal entry records are required, without affecting your business and life back home.

English Environment

English is the official language for business and law. Seamless educational transition for children to UK/US universities.

Two Core Visa Pathways

A

Retirement Visa

For those retired or with stable passive income. No need to work locally, suitable for long-term stay.

  • Proof of ZAR 37,000 (approx. USD 2,000) monthly passive income per person.
  • Sources can be pension, rental income, or dividends.
  • Spouse and dependent children can accompany.
B

Financially Independent

For High Net Worth Individuals. Obtain Permanent Residency (PR) directly.

  • Proof of global net assets of ZAR 12 million (approx. USD 650k).
  • One-time government fee of ZAR 120,000 required upon approval.
  • Grants direct PR status with all rights of citizenship except voting.
